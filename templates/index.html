Welcome!

OpenSpot is an app that allows customers to make reservations for businesses.


During the COVID-19 pandemic, a lot of businesses want to open up, but they don't want to have too many people at a time, and don't want any queues. This app is aimed at solving this problem as business owners can set the maximum amount of people they want in their store and the amount of time per person.

As a result, customers can simply book some time online and show up without having to queue!

OpenSpot is Free and Open Source Software. For support inquiries, contact: open.spot2020@gmail.com

OpenSpot data is stored on Google Cloud. OpenSpot source code is available at Github.
